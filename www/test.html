Consequences

Satellites

Satellites orbit the planet at high altitudes, so they are prone to damage to their electronics due to collisions with high-speed particles, reducing their useful life and affecting their navigation and telecommunications systems.

Electrical Networks

Storms can induce currents in power grids, overloading transformers and causing blackouts. These geomagnetic storms have the potential to seriously disrupt the electric infrastructure, leading to widespread outages and costly repairs.
